​Barberstarrs Barbershop is a premier grooming destination located in the heart of downtown Gresham, Oregon. Our team of skilled barbers are dedicated to providing top-notch haircuts and grooming services for men of all ages. Our comfortable and welcoming atmosphere makes Barberstarrs the perfect place to relax and get a fresh cut. We offer a range of services including traditional and modern haircuts, beard trims, hot towel shaves, and more. Our team is up-to-date on the latest techniques and styles, so you can trust that you'll leave our shop looking and feeling your best. If you're in the Portland or Gresham area, make an appointment at Barberstarrs Barbershop today and experience the best from our amazing and caring staff.
